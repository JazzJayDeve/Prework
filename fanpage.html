Queen

One of the Greatest Bands of All Time!

Bio

Queen. One of the biggest and most controversial bands of the 20th century. The band formed in London in 1970. The songwriters/musicians reached the pinnacle of their success in late 70's and early 80's with releasing 'Bohemian Rhapsody', Another One Bites the Dust','Radio Ga Ga','I Want to Break Free', and 'We Are the Champions' just to name a few. Their songs are played till this day for commercial advertisements and in althletic stadiums all over the world.
